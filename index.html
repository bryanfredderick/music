Facts About Music

Music Increases Happiness

Brain releases dopamine, a neurotransmitter that leads to increased feelings of happiness, excitement, and joy, when you listen to music you like.

Music Improves Performance in Running

Scientists found that runners who listened to fast or slow motivational music ran faster than runners who listened to calm music

Music Improves Sleep

 A study showed that listening to classical or relaxing music within an hour of going to bed significantly improves sleep, compared to listening to an audiobook or doing nothing before bed.
